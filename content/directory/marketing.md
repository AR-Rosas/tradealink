## Research & Planning

- [Google Keyword Planner](https://ads.google.com/home/tools/keyword-planner/) – Search demand (requires Ads account).
- [AnswerThePublic (Free)](https://answerthepublic.com/) – Question queries and topic ideas.
- [Meta Ad Library](https://www.facebook.com/ads/library/) – See competitors’ running ads.

## Content & Social

- [Buffer Free](https://buffer.com/) – Schedule posts to core networks.
- [Canva Free](https://www.canva.com/) – Social graphics, ads, and one-pagers.
- [Bitly Free](https://bitly.com/) – Link shortener with basic click stats.

## Email & Newsletters

- [Substack](https://substack.com/) – Free to start; subscriptions optional.
- [Beehiiv (Free)](https://www.beehiiv.com/) – Clean newsletter platform with growth features.
- [Buttondown (Free)](https://buttondown.email/) – Minimal newsletter tool.

## Analytics & Reporting

- [Google Analytics](https://analytics.google.com/) – Traffic and conversions.
- [Looker Studio](https://lookerstudio.google.com/) – Shareable dashboards.
- [UTM Builder](https://ga-dev-tools.web.app/ga4/campaign-url-builder/) – Tag links for campaign tracking.
