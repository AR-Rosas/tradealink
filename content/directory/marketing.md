## Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

- [Google Search Console](https://search.google.com/search-console/) – Free tool from Google to monitor site performance, indexing status, and fix technical issues.
- [Google Analytics (GA4)](https://analytics.google.com/analytics/web/) – Free web analytics to track traffic, user behavior, and conversion goals.
- [Google Keyword Planner](https://ads.google.com/home/tools/keyword-planner/) – Free tool within Google Ads for detailed keyword research and search volume estimates.
- [Ahrefs Free Tools](https://ahrefs.com/free-seo-tools) – Offers free tools for backlink checking, broken link checking, and limited site audit.
- [Ubersuggest](https://neilpatel.com/ubersuggest/) – Free daily searches for keyword suggestions, content ideas, and basic competitor analysis.
- [AnswerThePublic](https://answerthepublic.com/) – Visualizes common questions and phrases people search for around your topic (free limited searches).
- [Screaming Frog SEO Spider](https://www.screamingfrog.co.uk/seo-spider/) – Free version crawls up to 500 URLs to find broken links, missing meta data, and other SEO errors.
- [Yoast SEO](https://yoast.com/) – Free WordPress plugin for optimizing blog posts and pages for specific keywords.
- [Schema Markup Generator](https://technicalseo.com/tools/schema-markup-generator/) – Free tool to create JSON-LD schema for rich snippets.
- [Technical SEO](https://technicalseo.com/) – Collection of free technical SEO tools (robots.txt checker, site speed).

## Email Marketing

- [Mailchimp](https://mailchimp.com/) – Free plan for up to 500 contacts and 1,000 emails per month, basic automation included.
- [MailerLite](https://www.mailerlite.com/) – Free plan for up to 1,000 subscribers with drag-and-drop editor and segmentation.
- [ConvertKit](https://convertkit.com/) – Free landing pages and forms for up to 1,000 subscribers, focused on creators.
- [Substack](https://substack.com/) – Free platform for publishing newsletters and articles, monetizes via subscription fees.
- [Sendinblue (Brevo)](https://www.sendinblue.com/) – Free plan includes up to 300 emails per day and basic CRM features.
- [AWeber](https://www.aweber.com/) – Free plan for up to 500 subscribers with unlimited email sends.

## Social Media Management

- [Meta Business Suite](https://business.facebook.com/) – Free official tool to manage and schedule posts across Facebook and Instagram.
- [Buffer](https://buffer.com/) – Free plan for scheduling posts to up to 3 social accounts (limited posts per channel).
- [Later](https://later.com/) – Free plan for scheduling on Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, and more (limited posts per month).
- [Hootsuite](https://www.hootsuite.com/plans/free) – Free limited plan for managing 2 social accounts and basic scheduling.
- [TweetDeck (X Pro)](https://tweetdeck.twitter.com/) – Free, customizable dashboards for managing multiple Twitter (X) accounts and feeds.
- [Linktree](https://linktr.ee/) – Free 'link-in-bio' tool to consolidate all your important links for social profiles.
- [Canva](https://www.canva.com/) – Free template library for designing social media graphics, banners, and video clips.

## Content Creation & Writing

- [Google Docs](https://docs.google.com/) – Free word processor great for collaborative content drafting and sharing.
- [Notion](https://www.notion.so/) – Free workspace for content calendars, drafting, and organizing resources.
- [Grammarly](https://www.grammarly.com/) – Free browser extension for correcting spelling, grammar, and punctuation.
- [QuillBot](https://quillbot.com/) – Free paraphrasing and summarizing tool to refine content drafts.
- [Hemingway Editor](http://www.hemingwayapp.com/) – Free web app to simplify writing and improve readability.
- [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com/) – Free library of high-quality stock photos (no attribution required).
- [Pexels](https://www.pexels.com/) – Free stock images and video clips for content use.
- [Loom](https://www.loom.com/) – Free screen and video recording tool for creating tutorials and demonstrations (limited length).
- [Descript](https://www.descript.com/) – Free plan for basic video editing, transcription, and short clip creation.

## Advertising & CRO (Conversion Rate Optimization)

- [Google Ads](https://ads.google.com/) – Free account setup and tools (Keyword Planner) for running Search and Display ads (requires payment for running ads).
- [Meta Ads Manager](https://www.facebook.com/adsmanager/) – Free dashboard for creating, managing, and tracking Facebook and Instagram ad campaigns (requires payment for running ads).
- [Google Optimize (Deprecated, use GA4)](https://optimize.google.com/) – Previously free for A/B testing and personalization (functionality now moving to GA4 and Google Optimize 360).
- [Hotjar](https://www.hotjar.com/) – Free tier for heatmaps, recordings, and basic surveys to analyze user behavior on your site.
- [Microsoft Clarity](https://clarity.microsoft.com/) – Completely free tool for heatmaps, session recordings, and insights.

## Public Relations & Outreach

- [HARO (Help a Reporter Out)](https://www.helpareporter.com/) – Free service connecting journalists seeking sources with experts (useful for getting free press).
- [Muck Rack](https://muckrack.com/) – Free press release distribution and media monitoring (limited functionality).
- [Pressfarm](https://press.farm/) – Free starter search for finding journalists and media contacts.
- [Hunter.io](https://hunter.io/) – Free tool for finding email addresses associated with a website (limited searches per month).
- [Snov.io](https://snov.io/) – Free plan for basic email verification and finding email addresses (limited credits).
