## All-in-One Suites

- [PDF24 Tools](https://tools.pdf24.org/) – Free online suite with merge, compress, OCR, sign, edit, and convert PDFs.
- [iLovePDF](https://www.ilovepdf.com/) – Free online PDF merge, compress, convert, OCR, edit, and sign tools.
- [Smallpdf](https://smallpdf.com/) – Free PDF tools (merge, compress, convert, sign) with limited daily uses.
- [PDF2Go](https://www.pdf2go.com/) – Free browser PDF editor to edit, merge, compress, split, convert, and sign.
- [Sejda PDF](https://www.sejda.com/) – Free online PDF editor and toolset (limits on daily usage).
- [LightPDF](https://lightpdf.com/) – Free online PDF tools including convert, compress, merge, and OCR.

## Editing & Signing

- [DocHub](https://dochub.com/) – Free online PDF editor with annotation, form filling, and eSign features.
- [PDFescape](https://www.pdfescape.com/) – Free web PDF editor for annotations, form editing, and basic modifications.
- [Canva PDF Editor](https://www.canva.com/features/pdf-editor/) – Free browser PDF editor for quick edits and layouts.
- [FormSwift](https://formswift.com/pdf-editor) – Free online PDF editor with signing and simple edits.
- [BoldSign](https://boldsign.com/merge-pdf-online/) – Free browser-based PDF merging plus simple signing workflows.
- [PDFAid](https://pdfaid.com/) – Free online PDF editor with 50+ productivity tools including sign, merge, annotate.

## Convert & Compress

- [Online2PDF](https://online2pdf.com/) – Free all-in-one online PDF converter, merge, split, compress, unlock, and protect tool.
- [PDF.io](https://pdf.io/) – Essential online PDF utilities for merge, split, convert, compress without signup.
- [PDFgear](https://www.pdfgear.com/online-tools/) – Free online editor with merge, compress, edit, convert, OCR options.
- [DocuPub](https://www.docupub.com/) – Free online PDF converter and compression utility.
- [Free Document Maker](https://freedocumentmaker.com/) – Free online PDF tools for merge, compress, convert, and document generation.

## Desktop Tools

- [PDFsam Basic](https://pdfsam.org/) – Free desktop app to split, merge, rotate, and extract PDF pages.
- [PDF24 Creator](https://www.pdf24.org/en/) – Free Windows PDF creation & utility suite with merge, compress, sign, and edit.
