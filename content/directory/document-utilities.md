## Clean, Merge, Compress

- [PDF24 Tools](https://tools.pdf24.org/) – Reliable, free suite for merge, split, compress, and convert.
- [iLovePDF](https://www.ilovepdf.com/) – Quick browser-based PDF edits and exports.
- [Smallpdf](https://smallpdf.com/) – Compress, rotate, and convert PDFs in seconds.

## OCR (Make Scans Searchable)

- [Google Drive OCR](https://support.google.com/drive/answer/176692) – Open scans with Google Docs to extract text.
- [OCR.space](https://ocr.space/) – Free OCR for images and scans, decent accuracy.
- [Tesseract (CLI)](https://github.com/tesseract-ocr/tesseract) – Open-source OCR for batch jobs.

## Fill, Sign, and Share

- [DocHub](https://www.dochub.com/) – Fill and sign PDFs from Drive.
- [Adobe Acrobat Online](https://www.adobe.com/acrobat/online/sign-pdf.html) – Basic online e-sign tools.
- [Jotform Sign (Free)](https://www.jotform.com/products/sign/) – Simple e-sign with templates.

## Convert & Export

- [CloudConvert](https://cloudconvert.com/) – Convert between dozens of file formats.
- [Zamzar](https://www.zamzar.com/) – Quick file conversions in the browser.
- [LibreOffice](https://www.libreoffice.org/) – Free desktop suite for reliable DOCX ↔ PDF exports.
