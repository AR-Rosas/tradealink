## Invoicing & Payments

- [Wave](https://www.waveapps.com/) – Free invoicing and accounting for small agencies.
- [Invoice Ninja](https://www.invoiceninja.com/) – Free, open-source invoicing with client portal.
- [Stripe Payment Links](https://stripe.com/payments/links) – Shareable checkout links (processing fees apply).

## Contracts & Signatures

- [Notion + PDF Export](https://www.notion.so/) – Draft SOWs in Notion and export to PDF for client sign-off.
- [Jotform Sign (Free)](https://www.jotform.com/products/sign/) – E-signature with templates and limited free usage.
- [DocHub](https://www.dochub.com/) – Fill, sign, and send PDFs directly from Drive.

## Scheduling & Meetings

- [Calendly Free](https://calendly.com/) – Simple booking links for client calls and onboarding.
- [Cal.com](https://cal.com/) – Open-source scheduling with team routing.
- [Google Meet](https://meet.google.com/) – Free video calls with calendar invites.

## Bookkeeping & Admin

- [Wave Accounting](https://www.waveapps.com/) – Free bookkeeping, bank connections, and reports.
- [Google Sheets](https://sheets.google.com/) – Lightweight trackers for expenses, renewals, and payroll data.
- [Clockify](https://clockify.me/) – Time tracking across projects and clients.

## Client Knowledge Base

- [Notion Free](https://www.notion.so/) – SOPs, client workspaces, SLAs, and onboarding checklists.
- [Tally Forms](https://tally.so/) – Free intake forms for leads, feedback, and briefs.
- [Loom Free](https://www.loom.com/) – Async video walkthroughs for updates and handoffs.
