## Invoicing & Payments

- [Wave](https://www.waveapps.com/) – Free invoicing and basic accounting for small businesses and freelancers.
- [Zoho Invoice](https://www.zoho.com/invoice/) – Free invoicing tool with payment tracking and client management.
- [Invoice Ninja](https://www.invoiceninja.com/) – Open-source invoicing and expense tracking with free self-hosted option.
- [PayPal Invoicing](https://www.paypal.com/invoice) – Simple invoicing tied directly to PayPal payments.
- [Stripe Invoicing](https://stripe.com/invoicing) – Professional invoices integrated with Stripe payments and subscriptions.
- [Square Invoices](https://squareup.com/us/en/invoices) – Free invoicing with integrated payment acceptance.

## Accounting & Bookkeeping

- [FreshBooks](https://www.freshbooks.com/) – Easy invoicing and expense tracking with low-cost starter plans.
- [QuickBooks Online](https://quickbooks.intuit.com/) – Popular bookkeeping and invoicing software with scalable plans.
- [GnuCash](https://www.gnucash.org/) – Free open-source accounting software for bookkeeping and finances.
- [Zoho Books](https://www.zoho.com/books/) – Low-cost bookkeeping software with invoicing and expense tracking.

## Project & Task Management

- [Notion](https://www.notion.so/) – All-in-one workspace for contracts, scheduling, and operations tracking.
- [ClickUp](https://clickup.com/) – Project management with scheduling, docs, and task automation.
- [Trello](https://trello.com/) – Simple Kanban-based task and workflow management.
- [Asana](https://asana.com/) – Team task management and scheduling with a free tier.
- [Airtable](https://www.airtable.com/) – Spreadsheet-database hybrid for operations and tracking workflows.

## Scheduling & Meetings

- [Calendly](https://calendly.com/) – Free scheduling tool with automated meeting booking.
- [Google Calendar](https://calendar.google.com/) – Free calendar scheduling with sharing and reminders.

## Contracts & E-Signatures

- [Google Docs](https://docs.google.com/) – Free document creation for contracts and internal operations.
- [DocuSign](https://www.docusign.com/) – Industry-standard e-signature tool with affordable starter plans.
- [HelloSign](https://www.hellosign.com/) – Simple and low-cost electronic signature solution.
- [PandaDoc](https://www.pandadoc.com/) – Contracts, proposals, and e-signatures with a free tier.
- [Bonsai](https://www.hellobonsai.com/) – Freelancer-focused invoicing, contracts, and scheduling platform.

## Time Tracking

- [Clockify](https://clockify.me/) – Free time tracking for billing and productivity.
- [Toggl Track](https://toggl.com/track/) – Time tracking for invoicing and project management.

## Communication & Collaboration

- [Slack](https://slack.com/) – Team communication tool for everyday operations.
- [Microsoft Teams](https://www.microsoft.com/en/microsoft-teams/) – Collaboration, chat, and scheduling in one platform.
- [LibreOffice](https://www.libreoffice.org/) – Free office suite for documents, spreadsheets, and bookkeeping.

## CRM & Client Management

- [HubSpot CRM](https://www.hubspot.com/products/crm) – Free CRM for managing clients, deals, and communication.
- [Bitrix24](https://www.bitrix24.com/) – Free CRM and operations platform with tasks and invoicing.
- [Odoo](https://www.odoo.com/) – Modular business suite with free invoicing and CRM apps.
