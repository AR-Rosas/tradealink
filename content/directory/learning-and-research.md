## Academic & Scholarly Search

- [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/) – Free academic search engine for peer-reviewed papers, theses, and books.
- [JSTOR](https://www.jstor.org/) – Free access to articles and primary sources (limited access without institution).
- [Semantic Scholar](https://www.semanticscholar.org/) – AI-powered search tool for scientific literature with summaries.
- [CORE](https://core.ac.uk/) – Free access to millions of research papers harvested from repositories worldwide.
- [ResearchGate](https://www.researchgate.net/) – Social networking site for scientists and researchers to share work and ask questions.
- [PubMed](https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/) – Free database for biomedical literature and life science journals.

## General Research & Fact-Finding

- [Wikipedia](https://www.wikipedia.org/) – The world's largest free encyclopedia (always check primary sources for depth).
- [WolframAlpha](https://www.wolframalpha.com/) – Computational knowledge engine for math, science, and historical data queries.
- [DuckDuckGo](https://duckduckgo.com/) – Privacy-focused search engine for quick, unbiased web results.
- [Open Library](https://openlibrary.org/) – Free digital lending library for classic books and resources.
- [Internet Archive](https://archive.org/) – Free digital library of sites, books, audio, and video (Wayback Machine).
- [Statista](https://www.statista.com/) – Free access to high-quality statistics and market data (limited free charts).

## Writing, Citing & Grammar

- [Grammarly](https://www.grammarly.com/) – Free browser extension for correcting spelling and basic grammar.
- [QuillBot](https://quillbot.com/) – Free paraphrasing and summarizing tool (useful for checking comprehension).
- [Zotero](https://www.zotero.org/) – Free open-source tool to manage research materials and instantly create citations/bibliographies.
- [Mendeley](https://www.mendeley.com/) – Free reference manager and academic social network by Elsevier.
- [Google Docs](https://docs.google.com/) – Free word processor with collaborative features and basic citation tools.
- [Scribens](https://www.scribens.com/) – Free online grammar checker and proofreader.

## Data Analysis & Visualization

- [Kaggle](https://www.kaggle.com/) – Free platform for data science competitions, datasets, and notebooks (coding environment).
- [Google Public Data Explorer](https://www.google.com/publicdata/directory) – Free visualization and exploration tool for public data sets.
- [R-Project](https://www.r-project.org/) – Free software environment for statistical computing and graphics.
- [Python](https://www.python.org/) – Free programming language widely used for data science and analysis (requires setup).
- [Tableau Public](https://public.tableau.com/en-us/s/) – Free platform to create and share interactive data visualizations.
- [Google Sheets](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/u/0/) – Free spreadsheet program capable of basic data analysis and charting.

## Language Learning

- [Duolingo](https://www.duolingo.com/) – Free gamified app for learning over 40 languages.
- [Memrise](https://www.memrise.com/) – Free platform using mnemonics and native videos to teach vocabulary and phrases.
- [Tandem](https://www.tandem.net/) – Free language exchange app to practice with native speakers.
- [Google Translate](https://translate.google.com/) – Free translation tool for text, speech, and images.
- [Forvo](https://forvo.com/) – Free pronunciation guide for words spoken by native speakers.
