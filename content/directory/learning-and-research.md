## Research & Discovery

- [Perplexity](https://www.perplexity.ai/) – Ask research questions with citations.
- [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/) – Academic sources and citations.
- [Exploding Topics (Free)](https://explodingtopics.com/) – Early trend signals.

## Market & Competitors

- [Similarweb (Free)](https://www.similarweb.com/) – Traffic estimates and top pages.
- [BuiltWith](https://builtwith.com/) – See technologies used on competitor sites.
- [Wappalyzer](https://www.wappalyzer.com/) – Browser extension to detect stacks.

## Monitoring & Alerts

- [Google Alerts](https://www.google.com/alerts) – Track competitor and topic mentions by email.
- [Feedly (Free)](https://feedly.com/) – Aggregate industry blogs and newsletters.
- [Talkwalker Alerts](https://www.talkwalker.com/alerts) – Brand/topic monitoring alternative.

## Notes & Knowledge

- [Notion Free](https://www.notion.so/) – Team notebooks, wikis, and research hubs.
- [Obsidian](https://obsidian.md/) – Local markdown knowledge base with backlinks.
- [Readwise Reader](https://readwise.io/read) – Save articles and highlights, resurface with spaced repetition.
