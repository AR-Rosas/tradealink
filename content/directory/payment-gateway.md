## All-in-One Processors (API & E-commerce)

- [Stripe](https://stripe.com/) – Comprehensive API for online payments, subscriptions, and global business banking (favored by developers).
- [PayPal](https://www.paypal.com/us/business) – Widely recognized platform for online payments, invoicing, and peer-to-peer transfers.
- [Braintree (by PayPal)](https://www.braintreegateway.com/) – Full-stack payment platform for accepting global payments and recurring billing.
- [Square](https://squareup.com/) – All-in-one payment solution for online stores, in-person POS, and invoicing.
- [Adyen](https://www.adyen.com/) – Global payment platform supporting a massive number of payment methods and market expansion.
- [Checkout.com](https://www.checkout.com/) – Cloud-based payment processing solution focused on reliability and complex setups.
- [2Checkout (Verifone)](https://www.2checkout.com/) – Global monetization platform supporting e-commerce, subscriptions, and digital goods.
- [Klarna](https://www.klarna.com/business/) – Payment solution specializing in "Buy Now, Pay Later" (BNPL) services.
- [Mollie](https://www.mollie.com/) – European-focused payment service provider for easy, integrated online payments.
- [Midtrans](https://midtrans.com/) – Indonesian payment gateway specializing in local payment methods.

## Subscription & Recurring Billing

- [Chargebee](https://www.chargebee.com/) – Comprehensive subscription management and recurring billing platform.
- [Recurly](https://recurly.com/) – Subscription billing platform focusing on reducing churn and managing complex plans.
- [Zuora](https://www.zuora.com/) – Enterprise-level subscription management and commerce platform.
- [GoCardless](https://gocardless.com/) – Specializes in collecting recurring payments (Direct Debit/ACH) globally.

## Creator & Digital Product Platforms (MoR)
*These platforms act as a Merchant of Record, handling global sales tax, VAT, and compliance.*

- [Lemon Squeezy](https://www.lemonsqueezy.com/) – **Merchant of Record (MoR)** for selling digital products, handling sales tax and compliance globally.
- [Whop](https://whop.com/) – Marketplace and payment platform focused on selling access to communities, software, and digital memberships.
- [Paddle](https://www.paddle.com/) – **Merchant of Record (MoR)** platform handling billing, taxes, and compliance for software sellers.
- [Gumroad](https://gumroad.com/) – Popular platform for creators to sell digital products, courses, and memberships directly to their audience.
- [Polar](https://polar.sh/) – Platform for selling access to GitHub repositories, private communities, and digital memberships (great for open-source funding).
- [Thrivecart](https://thrivecart.com/) – Powerful checkout platform optimized for conversions, upsells, and digital product funnels (one-time fee).
- [SendOwl](https://www.sendowl.com/) – E-commerce solution for selling digital products, subscriptions, and memberships quickly.
- [FastSpring](https://fastspring.com/) – Full-service e-commerce and MoR platform for digital products and software.

## Invoicing & Simple Payments

- [Wave Invoicing](https://www.waveapps.com/) – Free invoicing and basic accounting for freelancers and small businesses.
- [FreshBooks](https://www.freshbooks.com/) – Accounting and invoicing software popular with small service businesses (free trial).
- [Zoho Invoice](https://www.zoho.com/invoice/) – Free invoicing tool integrated with the larger Zoho suite.
- [Harvest](https://www.getharvest.com/) – Time tracking and invoicing tool for service businesses (limited free tier).
- [QuickBooks Payments](https://quickbooks.intuit.com/payments/) – Payment processing integrated directly into QuickBooks accounting software.
- [Bill.com](https://www.bill.com/) – Automates accounts payable and receivable for small and midsize businesses.
- [Square Invoices](https://squareup.com/invoices) – Easy-to-use invoicing tool from Square, integrated with their payment processing.

## Local & Regional Gateways

- [Razorpay](https://razorpay.com/) – Leading payment gateway in India, supporting UPI, net banking, and international cards.
- [Paytm Payment Gateway](https://business.paytm.com/) – Popular payment solution in India for online and mobile payments.
- [Mercado Pago](https://www.mercadopago.com.ar/) – Leading payment solution in Latin America (part of MercadoLibre).
- [Alipay](https://global.alipay.com/) – Widely used Chinese payment method for global and local transactions.
- [WeChat Pay](https://pay.weixin.qq.com/) – Mobile payment service dominant in China.
- [eWAY](https://www.eway.com.au/) – Leading payment gateway in Australia and New Zealand.
- [Paysera](https://www.paysera.com/) – Lithuanian payment system focusing on SEPA/SWIFT payments and e-wallets in Europe.
- [Interac](https://www.interac.ca/) – Canadian debit card service, widely used for e-commerce and peer-to-peer.
- [Sepa Direct Debit](https://www.europeanpaymentscouncil.eu/what-we-do/sepa-direct-debit) – European standard for recurring bank transfers (not a gateway, but a method).

## High-Risk & Niche Gateways

- [Payoneer](https://www.payoneer.com/) – Global payment service for freelancers and cross-border business payments.
- [Skrill](https://www.skrill.com/) – E-wallet and payment service often used for gaming and global money transfers.
- [Neteller](https://www.neteller.com/) – E-money transfer service popular in online gambling and trading sectors.
- [Authorize.Net](https://www.authorize.net/) – Veteran gateway for mid-market and enterprise businesses, supports complex needs.
- [PayU](https://www.payu.com/) – Payment processing focused on emerging markets (India, Latin America, Eastern Europe).

## Cryptocurrency & DeFi Payments

- [Coinbase Commerce](https://commerce.coinbase.com/) – Simple solution for businesses to accept Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other cryptocurrencies.
- [BitPay](https://bitpay.com/) – Leading platform for processing crypto payments for e-commerce and invoices.
- [CoinPayments](https://www.coinpayments.net/) – Supports over 2,000 cryptocurrencies for payment processing.
- [NOWPayments](https://nowpayments.io/) – Crypto payment gateway for e-commerce, invoices, and mass payments.
- [TripleA](https://triple-a.io/) – Crypto payment gateway with instant conversion to local currency.

## Point-of-Sale (POS) & Retail

- [Clover](https://www.clover.com/) – Integrated POS system and payment processing for restaurants and retail.
- [Shopify POS](https://www.shopify.com/pos) – POS system that syncs with Shopify online stores.
- [Toast](https://pos.toasttab.com/) – Cloud-based POS system designed specifically for the restaurant industry.
- [Lightspeed](https://www.lightspeedhq.com/) – Cloud POS for retail and restaurants, specializing in complex inventories.
