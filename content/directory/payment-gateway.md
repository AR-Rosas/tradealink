## Quick Checkout Links

- [Stripe Payment Links](https://stripe.com/payments/links) – Create shareable links for one-time or recurring payments.
- [Square Online Checkout](https://squareup.com/us/en/online-checkout) – Simple, shareable checkout without a full store.
- [PayPal Buttons](https://www.paypal.com/buttons/) – Generate buy-now or donate buttons fast.

## Invoicing + Card Payments

- [Wave](https://www.waveapps.com/) – Free invoicing with card payments (fees apply).
- [Invoice Ninja](https://www.invoiceninja.com/) – Client portal, invoices, recurring billing (open-source).
- [Zoho Invoice](https://www.zoho.com/invoice/) – Free invoicing with multiple gateways.

## Subscriptions & Memberships

- [Lemon Squeezy](https://www.lemonsqueezy.com/) – Subscriptions, licensing, EU VAT handled.
- [Paddle](https://www.paddle.com/) – Global checkout (great for SaaS), handles taxes.
- [Gumroad](https://gumroad.com/) – Quick digital product sales.
