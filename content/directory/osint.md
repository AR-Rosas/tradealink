## Company & Domain Footprints

- [WHOIS Lookup (ICANN)](https://lookup.icann.org/) – Domain registration details.
- [Wayback Machine](https://web.archive.org/) – Historical snapshots of websites.
- [BuiltWith](https://builtwith.com/) – Identify technologies used on a site.
- [Wappalyzer](https://www.wappalyzer.com/) – Browser extension to detect stacks.

## People & Email Discovery

- [Hunter.io (Free)](https://hunter.io/) – Find patterns and verify emails.
- [Clearbit Connect (Free)](https://connect.clearbit.com/) – Company and contact context in Gmail.
- [Google Advanced Search](https://www.google.com/advanced_search) – Narrow results; pair with site: and filetype: operators.

## Network & Infrastructure

- [SecurityTrails](https://securitytrails.com/) – DNS, subdomains, and related assets (free tier).
- [Censys](https://search.censys.io/) – Internet-wide scan data (research; handle with care).
- [Shodan](https://www.shodan.io/) – Indexed devices/services on the internet (research; handle with care).

## Social & News Monitoring

- [Google Alerts](https://www.google.com/alerts) – Track companies, execs, and keywords.
- [Talkwalker Alerts](https://www.talkwalker.com/alerts) – Alternative for mentions.
- [Reddit Search](https://www.reddit.com/search/) – Discover discussions and feedback.

> Note: Always use OSINT ethically. Respect terms of service, privacy, and local regulations.
