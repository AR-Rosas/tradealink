## Lead Capture & Intake

- [Tally Forms](https://tally.so/) – Free, polished intake forms with hidden fields and notifications.
- [Google Forms](https://forms.google.com/) – Simple intake with Sheets backend for tracking.
- [Jotform](https://www.jotform.com/) – Free plan for advanced forms, e-signatures, and simple automation.
- [Typeform](https://www.typeform.com/) – Create interactive forms and surveys (limited free tier).
- [Notion Forms (Feather.so)](https://feather.so/) – Create forms that write directly to Notion databases.

## Contracts & Proposals

- [Notion](https://www.notion.so/) – Create, manage, and share detailed proposal and contract templates.
- [Canva Docs](https://www.canva.com/docs/) – Design clean, branded proposals with share links.
- [PandaDoc Free eSign](https://www.pandadoc.com/) – Send basic proposals for signature (free limited).
- [DocuSign Free](https://www.docusign.com/) – Send up to 3 documents for e-signature (free tier).
- [HelloSign (Dropbox Sign)](https://www.hellosign.com/) – Free plan for sending one document per month for signature.
- [Google Docs](https://docs.google.com/) – Collaborative and free document creation for scope changes and notes.

## Invoicing & Payments

- [Wave](https://www.waveapps.com/) – Free invoicing, basic accounting, and expense tracking for small businesses.
- [PayPal Invoicing](https://www.paypal.com/invoice) – Send professional invoices directly linked to PayPal payments (free to send).
- [Stripe Invoicing](https://stripe.com/invoicing) – Create and send professional invoices (transaction fees apply, no monthly fee).
- [Zoho Invoice](https://www.zoho.com/invoice/) – Free invoicing for single user with payment tracking.

## Time & Project Management

- [ClickUp Free](https://clickup.com/) – Tasks, timelines, docs, and basic time tracking in one place (free tier).
- [Trello Free](https://trello.com/) – Kanban boards for project workflows and content calendars.
- [Asana](https://asana.com/) – Task management for individuals and small teams (free up to 15 users).
- [Todoist](https://todoist.com/) – Simple, effective task management for daily work (generous free tier).
- [Clockify](https://clockify.me/) – Free time tracking tool for monitoring billable hours per client.
- [Toggl Track](https://toggl.com/track/) – Simple time tracking for billing and productivity (free basic plan).

## Portfolio & Presence

- [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/) – Free static site hosting for technical portfolios and personal sites.
- [Notion Sites](https://www.notion.so/) – Fast, simple portfolio pages and case studies using Notion's public share feature.
- [Framer](https://www.framer.com/) – Polished portfolio or simple client sites (free starter plan).
- [Behance](https://www.behance.net/) – Free platform to showcase creative projects and design work.
- [Dribbble](https://dribbble.com/) – Free profile for displaying small design snippets and portfolio links.
- [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/) – Professional networking and portfolio presentation.

## Communication & Demo

- [Loom](https://www.loom.com/) – Async video updates and screen walkthroughs for clients (free limited recording).
- [Slack](https://slack.com/) – Client channels for faster feedback loops (free limited history).
- [Google Meet](https://meet.google.com/) – Free video calls with calendar invites and screen sharing.
- [Calendly](https://calendly.com/) – Free scheduling tool for booking client meetings (one event type free).
- [Zoom Basic](https://zoom.us/) – Free plan for basic video conferencing (40-minute limit).
