## Lead Capture & Intake

- [Tally Forms](https://tally.so/) – Free, polished intake forms with hidden fields and notifications.
- [Google Forms](https://forms.google.com/) – Simple intake with Sheets backend for tracking.
- [Notion Forms (Feather.so)](https://feather.so/) – Create forms that write directly to Notion.

## Proposals & Scoping

- [Notion](https://www.notion.so/) – Share proposal templates with timelines and scope blocks.
- [Canva Docs](https://www.canva.com/docs/) – Clean, branded proposals with share links.
- [PandaDoc Free eSign](https://www.pandadoc.com/) – Send basic proposals for signature.

## Portfolio & Presence

- [Notion Sites](https://www.notion.so/) – Fast portfolio pages and case studies.
- [Framer Free](https://www.framer.com/) – Polished portfolio or simple client sites.
- [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/) – Free static site hosting for technical portfolios.

## Time, Tasks & Delivery

- [ClickUp Free](https://clickup.com/) – Tasks, timelines, and docs in one place.
- [Trello Free](https://trello.com/) – Kanban boards for projects and content calendars.
- [Clockify](https://clockify.me/) – Track billable time per client and project.

## Communication

- [Loom Free](https://www.loom.com/) – Async updates and walkthroughs for clients.
- [Slack Free](https://slack.com/) – Client channels for faster feedback loops.
- [Google Meet](https://meet.google.com/) – Video calls with calendar invites.
