## Static Hosting (Free Tiers)

- [Netlify](https://www.netlify.com/) – CI deploys, forms, redirects; generous free tier.
- [Vercel](https://vercel.com/) – Great for Next.js/Nuxt; instant previews.
- [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/) – Free static hosting from repos.

## Traditional Hosting

- [DigitalOcean](https://www.digitalocean.com/) – Simple droplets (VPS) for apps and databases.
- [Hetzner](https://www.hetzner.com/) – Affordable VPS and dedicated servers.
- [DreamHost](https://www.dreamhost.com/) – Budget-friendly shared hosting.

## Domains & DNS

- [Cloudflare](https://www.cloudflare.com/) – DNS, CDN, SSL, security rules (free).
- [Namecheap](https://www.namecheap.com/) – Domains with decent DNS.
- [Google Domains (Legacy)](https://domains.google/) – Some clients still use it; consider migration.
