## AI Assistants

- [ChatGPT (Free)](https://chat.openai.com/) – Draft emails, proposals, briefs, and quick content for clients.
- [Claude](https://claude.ai/) – Great for long documents, research summaries, and structured writing.
- [Perplexity](https://www.perplexity.ai/) – Research assistant with source-backed answers for client work.

## Workflow & Automation

- [Zapier (Free Plan)](https://zapier.com/pricing) – Connect your CRM, email, and project tools to automate simple workflows.
- [Make (Free Plan)](https://www.make.com/) – Visual automations to move data between tools and trigger reports.
- [Pabbly Connect](https://www.pabbly.com/connect/) – Budget-friendly automation for agencies managing multiple client accounts.

## Content & Creative

- [Canva Free](https://www.canva.com/) – Quick social graphics, simple ads, and client decks.
- [Figma Starter](https://www.figma.com/) – Collaborative wireframes and UI mockups for client websites or apps.
- [Descript Free](https://www.descript.com/) – Edit client videos and podcasts using text.

## Data & Reporting

- [Looker Studio](https://lookerstudio.google.com/) – Build shareable client dashboards from multiple data sources.
- [Google Analytics](https://analytics.google.com/) – Track website and landing page performance for clients.
- [Microsoft Clarity](https://clarity.microsoft.com/) – Free heatmaps and session recordings to spot UX issues.

## Internal Ops

- [Notion Free](https://www.notion.so/) – SOPs, client workspaces, and internal knowledge base in one place.
- [Trello Free](https://trello.com/) – Boards for tracking deliverables and content calendars.
- [Clockify](https://clockify.me/) – Time tracking across clients and projects to understand profitability.
