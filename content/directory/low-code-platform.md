## Internal Tools

- [Retool](https://retool.com/) – Drag-and-drop dashboards on top of databases/APIs.
- [Budibase](https://budibase.com/) – Open-source internal tools; self-hostable.
- [Appsmith](https://www.appsmith.com/) – Open-source low-code for CRUD apps.

## App Builders

- [Glide](https://www.glideapps.com/) – Build apps from Sheets; great for directories.
- [AppSheet](https://www.appsheet.com/) – Google’s no-code apps on Sheets/Drive.
- [Bubble](https://bubble.io/) – Powerful no-code web apps.

## Automation & Backend

- [N8n](https://n8n.io/) – Open-source workflow automation.
- [Supabase](https://supabase.com/) – Postgres + auth + storage for quick backends.
- [Firebase](https://firebase.google.com/) – Hosting, auth, and realtime DB.
