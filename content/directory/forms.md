## Embeddable Form Builders

- [Tally](https://tally.so/) – Free, polished forms; popups, hidden fields, webhooks.
- [Google Forms](https://forms.google.com/) – Fast forms; Sheets backend for tracking.
- [Typeform (Free)](https://www.typeform.com/) – Conversational forms; limited free tier.

## Advanced Features

- [Jotform](https://www.jotform.com/) – Conditional logic, PDFs, signatures.
- [Paperform](https://paperform.co/) – Branded forms with calculations.
- [Formspree](https://formspree.io/) – Form submissions for static sites.

## Storage & Automation

- [Make](https://www.make.com/) – Send form data to CRMs and docs.
- [Zapier](https://zapier.com/) – Connect forms to email, Sheets, Notion.
- [Notion](https://www.notion.so/) – Save submissions into shared databases.
