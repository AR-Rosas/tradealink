## User-Friendly & General Builders

- [Tally](https://tally.so/) – Minimalist UI with surprisingly deep logic, hidden fields, and automation hooks.
- [Typeform](https://www.typeform.com/) – Highly engaging, conversational forms with rich branching and scoring.
- [Jotform](https://www.jotform.com/) – Versatile form builder with approvals, conditional logic, and document generation.
- [Paperform](https://paperform.co/) – Forms that double as landing pages and support advanced conditional rules.
- [Cognito Forms](https://www.cognitoforms.com/) – Free tier with repeating sections, calculations, and workflow rules.
- [Wufoo](https://www.wufoo.com/) – Mature form builder with payments, logic, and reporting.
- [Formsite](https://www.formsite.com/) – Nested logic, calculations, and secure workflows for complex surveys.

## Developer & API-Focused

- [Form.io](https://form.io/) – API-first forms platform for developers: JSON schema, APIs, and complex validation.
- [Formcarry](https://formcarry.com/) – Simple form endpoints with advanced validations for developers.
- [OhMyForm](https://ohmyform.com/) – Open-source and self-hostable alternative to Typeform with deep customization.

## Surveys, Research & Feedback

- [SurveyMonkey](https://www.surveymonkey.com/) – Survey-first tool with advanced skip logic and analytics.
- [LimeSurvey](https://www.limesurvey.org/) – Open-source survey platform designed for complex question routing.
- [Qualtrics](https://www.qualtrics.com/) – Research-grade surveys and analytics (free student/basic plans available).

## Ecosystem & Database Integration

- [Airtable Forms](https://airtable.com/forms) – Database-connected forms that trigger automations and workflows.
- [HubSpot Forms](https://www.hubspot.com/products/forms) – CRM-connected forms with automation triggers and smart fields.
- [Microsoft Forms](https://forms.microsoft.com/) – Quick surveys with branching that integrate deeply with Microsoft 365.
- [Zoho Forms](https://www.zoho.com/forms/ – Powerful rules and approval flows that tie into the Zoho ecosystem.
- [Formstack](https://www.formstack.com/) – Enterprise workflows, integrations, and secure data handling.

## WordPress Specific (for reference)

- [Gravity Forms](https://www.gravityforms.com/) – Powerful WordPress ecosystem with advanced logic and many add-ons.
- [Fabrica Forms (WordPress)](https://wordpress.org/plugins/fabrica-forms/) – WP plugin for conditional visibility and calculated fields.
