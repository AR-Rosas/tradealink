## Site Builders (Visual)

- [WordPress.org](https://wordpress.org/) – Free, open-source CMS with huge plugin ecosystem and easy setup.
- [Webflow](https://webflow.com/) – Visual CMS with design flexibility and free starter plan.
- [Squarespace](https://www.squarespace.com/) – Intuitive all-in-one CMS with stylish templates (free trial).
- [Wix](https://www.wix.com/) – User-friendly drag-and-drop site builder with free plan.
- [Weebly](https://www.weebly.com/) – Simple website builder with free tier and ecommerce support.
- [Blogger](https://www.blogger.com/) – Free Google-hosted blogging platform.

## Headless & API-First

- [Strapi](https://strapi.io/) – Open-source headless CMS with API flexibility.
- [Contentful](https://www.contentful.com/) – Cloud headless CMS with generous free tier.
- [Sanity](https://www.sanity.io/) – Headless CMS with real-time collaboration and free tier.
- [Directus](https://directus.io/) – Free data-first headless CMS that works with SQL databases.
- [Craft CMS](https://craftcms.com/) – Flexible CMS with strong developer focus and free tier.

## Static & Flat-File

- [Hugo](https://gohugo.io/) – Free static site generator ideal for fast simple sites.
- [Jekyll](https://jekyllrb.com/) – Static site generator that works with GitHub Pages.
- [Publii](https://getpublii.com/) – Desktop-based static CMS great for fast, secure client sites.
- [Grav](https://getgrav.org/) – Flat-file CMS that’s fast, simple, and free.
- [Netlify CMS](https://www.netlifycms.org/) – Open-source CMS for static sites with Git workflow.

## Enterprise & Advanced

- [Drupal](https://www.drupal.org/) – Powerful open-source CMS for complex, scalable sites.
- [Joomla](https://www.joomla.org/) – Flexible, open-source CMS for more advanced sites.
- [TYPO3](https://typo3.org/) – Enterprise-grade open-source CMS with robust features.
- [Magento Open Source](https://magento.com/products/magento-open-source) – Free ecommerce CMS for larger online stores.
- [SilverStripe](https://www.silverstripe.org/) – Open-source CMS with flexible content modeling.
- [ModX](https://modx.com/) – Free CMS focused on flexibility and design freedom.

## Publishing & Newsletters

- [Ghost](https://ghost.org/) – Clean, modern CMS focused on blogging and newsletters.
- [Substack](https://substack.com/) – Free publishing platform for newsletters and articles.
- [Medium](https://medium.com/) – Simple publishing platform with built-in audience.
