## Design Tools

- [Figma Starter](https://www.figma.com/) – Collaborative design and prototyping.
- [Penpot](https://penpot.app/) – Open-source design/prototyping.
- [Canva Free](https://www.canva.com/) – Quick social and marketing visuals.

## Icons & Fonts

- [Iconify](https://iconify.design/) – Unified icon sets (Lucide, Heroicons, etc.).
- [Heroicons](https://heroicons.com/) – Free MIT-licensed icons.
- [Google Fonts](https://fonts.google.com/) – Free web fonts with easy embeds.

## Images & Illustrations

- [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com/) – Free photos (check license).
- [Pexels](https://www.pexels.com/) – Free stock photos and videos.
- [unDraw](https://undraw.co/illustrations) – Open-source illustrations.

## Optimization

- [Squoosh](https://squoosh.app/) – Compress and convert images in browser.
- [SVGOMG](https://jakearchibald.github.io/svgomg/) – Optimize SVGs.
- [TinyPNG](https://tinypng.com/) – PNG/JPG compression.
