## Coding & CS

- [freeCodeCamp](https://www.freecodecamp.org/) – Learn to code with free interactive lessons and projects.
- [The Odin Project](https://www.theodinproject.com/) – Free full-stack web development curriculum with projects.
- [Codecademy](https://www.codecademy.com/catalog/subject/all?price=free) – Free beginner coding lessons.
- [Replit](https://replit.com/) – Free online coding environment to build projects.
- [JavaScript30](https://javascript30.com/) – Free 30-day vanilla JavaScript coding challenge.
- [Teach Yourself CS](https://teachyourselfcs.com/) – Free curriculum to self-study computer science.
- [Rustlings](https://github.com/rust-lang/rustlings) – Free Rust programming hands-on exercises.
- [Rails Tutorial](https://www.railstutorial.org/) – Free Ruby on Rails app development book.
- [SoloLearn](https://www.sololearn.com/) – Free bite-sized coding lessons.

## University & Academic

- [Khan Academy](https://www.khanacademy.org/) – Free courses in math, science, and more with practice exercises.
- [Coursera](https://www.coursera.org/) – Free audit access to courses from top universities.
- [edX](https://www.edx.org/) – Free access to university courses worldwide.
- [MIT OpenCourseWare](https://ocw.mit.edu/) – Free MIT course materials from real classes.
- [Harvard Online](https://online-learning.harvard.edu/) – Free courses from Harvard University.
- [Stanford Online](https://online.stanford.edu/courses) – Free and paid Stanford courses.
- [Open Yale Courses](https://oyc.yale.edu/) – Free access to Yale course lectures.
- [Saylor Academy](https://www.saylor.org/) – Free self-paced college-level courses.
- [OpenLearn](https://www.open.edu/openlearn/) – Free courses from The Open University.
- [Academic Earth](https://academicearth.org/) – Free online college courses.

## Tech Skills & Cloud

- [Microsoft Learn](https://learn.microsoft.com/) – Free guided learning paths for Microsoft tech.
- [AWS Training](https://aws.amazon.com/training/) – Free cloud computing skills and labs.
- [IBM SkillsBuild](https://skillsbuild.org/) – Free tech, data, and career development.
- [Google Digital Garage](https://learndigital.withgoogle.com/digitalgarage) – Free digital marketing and career skills.
- [DataCamp](https://www.datacamp.com/free) – Free intro to data science and Python/R.
- [Qwiklabs](https://www.qwiklabs.com/) – Free cloud labs to build real skills with occasional credits.
- [Google AI](https://ai.google/education/) – Free resources to learn AI and machine learning.
- [OpenAI Learn](https://platform.openai.com/learn) – Free guides to build with AI tools.
- [Kaggle Learn](https://www.kaggle.com/learn) – Free data science and machine learning lessons.

## Career & Portfolio

- [LinkedIn Learning](https://www.linkedin.com/learning/) – Free trial for professional skills and portfolio-building.
- [Interview Cake](https://www.interviewcake.com/) – Free algorithm practice and career interview prep.
- [LeetCode](https://leetcode.com/problemset/all/) – Free coding problems for skill building.
- [HackerRank](https://www.hackerrank.com/) – Free coding challenges to build programming portfolios.
- [Exercism](https://exercism.org/) – Free coding practice and mentorship.
- [HubSpot Academy](https://academy.hubspot.com/) – Free marketing, sales, and service certifications.
- [SEMrush Academy](https://www.semrush.com/academy/) – Free SEO and digital marketing courses.
- [Stripe Learn](https://stripe.com/learn) – Free payments and business product tutorials.
- [Dev.to](https://dev.to/) – Community articles and tutorials for developers.
- [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/) – Free site hosting to showcase portfolio projects.

## Creative & Design

- [Canva Design School](https://www.canva.com/learn/design-school/) – Free design tutorials and tips.
- [Blender Guru](https://www.blenderguru.com/tutorials) – Free Blender 3D modeling and animation lessons.
- [Behance](https://www.behance.net/) – Free platform to showcase creative portfolio.
- [Dribbble](https://dribbble.com/) – Free design portfolio exposure.
- [CreativeLive](https://www.creativelive.com/free) – Free creative and business skill classes.
