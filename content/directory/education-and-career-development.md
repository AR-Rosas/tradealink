## Courses & Pathways (Free)

- [freeCodeCamp](https://www.freecodecamp.org/) – Full web dev curriculum + certifications.
- [The Odin Project](https://www.theodinproject.com/) – Open-source web dev pathway.
- [CS50](https://cs50.harvard.edu/) – Harvard’s intro CS course.

## Platforms & Libraries

- [edX](https://www.edx.org/) – University courses (audit free).
- [Coursera](https://www.coursera.org/) – Many courses free to audit.
- [MIT OpenCourseWare](https://ocw.mit.edu/) – Free MIT course materials.

## Portfolios & Resumes

- [Notion](https://www.notion.so/) – Create a simple portfolio and resume page.
- [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/) – Host developer portfolios for free.
- [Canva](https://www.canva.com/) – Resume and portfolio templates.

## Job Search & Tracking

- [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/) – Networking and jobs.
- [Indeed](https://www.indeed.com/) – Job search and alerts.
- [Notion Job Tracker Template](https://www.notion.so/templates) – Track applications and interviews.
